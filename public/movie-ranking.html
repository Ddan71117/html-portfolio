The Best Movies According to Daniel

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

Wayne's World

This movie is a cult classic and it's jokes are timeless and very quotable.

The Matrix

Though outdated by todays standards, this movie was arguably considered
revolution in filmography where special affects are conserned as well as an
original Sci-fi story.

Pulp Fiction

The abstract plot line that eventually came together in the end made this
movie brilliant. The dark humor and vulgarity was also memorable and quotable.
